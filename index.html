Whats the Story?

Something about doors makes my mind transfix in amaze at the beauty and elegance that it radiates. The architecture of doors have evolved and have taken up a style from one country to another. They make abstract statements about the country's history and past. This in turn reflects about the people of the country.
I believe that a beautiful door, which stands ajar casting a dark shadow says a lot about life. The darkness and the beauty of the the exterior and interior show the darker and brighter side of life. How one has to pass the casting darkness to reach the brighter interiors. Architecturally doors have unfolded very creatively and have left me spellbound. I would like to reflect the essence of OpenDoors manifesting a welcoming nature and in turn leaving a mark.

HOW DO I
WORK ?

Discover

This is the strategy phase which involves understanding the project brief and the needs of the client. It is the most important stage as you are strategizing how you are going to go about the entire project, setting a timeline, and long term agendas for meeting the goals client and executing the project.

Research and Define

This phase involves intensive research : User research, Domain Research, Competitive Analysis etc and finally defining your problem statement and product statement. The product statement and problem statement keeps getting iterates as you continue your research and get insights from your target audience.

Design

This is the exciting stage when you can start visualising the solution. Based on the analysis if the research you can build a potential conceptual model of your solution. Your deliverables become more clear. User flows , scenarios and use cases are build to hypothetically validate your solution. This is followed by wireframing or system flow based on the kind of solution.

Build

By now the skeleton of your product is ready. It's time to prototype. Prototyping is when the work becomes more dextrous. It is a combination of both hardware and software depending on the kind of solution. By the end of this stage you have a mcok working solution in your hand.

User Testing

This is the most important phase of the project. It is at this stage when you will know the success of your product.Usability testing your product with the target audience helps you to fill the gaps. It also gives certain insights from the userâ€™s point of view which hasnâ€™t been catered to.

Validate

Validate is a step that happens parallelly right from the beginning of the project. This step ensures you are in the right direction. It is constant validation from your target audience if the insights you are getting at every stage is valuable or not. If valuable, it is a green signal to go ahead. If not valuable, it means that there is a need to go one step back and rethink.

Iterate

Iterate is tweaking and changing your insights, scenrios, solution or any other form of output after any stage that needs reworking. When the validation returns false, you iterate. It helps to ensure that our solution is as user centric as possible in order to create the best experience.
